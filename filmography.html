Bruce Willis Filmography

Welcome to the comprehensive filmography of the legendary Bruce Willis! With a career spanning decades, Bruce has given us countless memorable performances. Here, we've highlighted some of the most significant films of his illustrious career.

Highlighted Films:

  • Die Hard (1988)

    Arguably Bruce's most iconic role, he plays NYPD officer John McClane, who takes on terrorists in a Los Angeles skyscraper on Christmas Eve.

  • Pulp Fiction (1994)

    Directed by Quentin Tarantino, Bruce plays the role of Butch Coolidge, a boxer with a story that intertwines with other colorful characters.

  • The Sixth Sense (1999)

    In this supernatural thriller, Bruce portrays Dr. Malcolm Crowe, a child psychologist helping a young boy who claims he can see dead people.

  • Unbreakable (2000)

    Reuniting with "The Sixth Sense" director M. Night Shyamalan, Bruce plays David Dunn, a man who discovers he has superhuman abilities.

  • Sin City (2005)

    In this stylized neo-noir film, Bruce takes on the role of Hartigan, a cop with a strong sense of justice.

  • Moonlighting (1985-1989)

    Before he was a big-screen action hero, Bruce starred as David Addison Jr. in this popular TV series, showcasing his comedic timing and chemistry with co-star Cybill Shepherd.

Other Notable Films:

  • Die Hard 2 (1990)
  • Die Hard with a Vengeance (1995)
  • 12 Monkeys (1995)
  • Armageddon (1998)
  • The Fifth Element (1997)
  • RED (2010)
  • Looper (2012)

Awards and Recognition

Throughout his career, Bruce Willis has received numerous awards and nominations, celebrating his versatility and talent as an actor.
